Dr. Carl Sagan

Astronomer, writer, and communicator

A timeline of Carl's life

  • 1934: Born in Brooklyn, NY
  • 1955: Earns a bachelor's in Physics from the University of Chicago
  • 1960: Publishes his dissertation "Physical Studies of Planets"
  • 1960: Worked as a Miller fellow at UC Berkeley
  • 1962: Began teaching at Harvard
  • 1968: Transferred to Cornell University
  • 1972: Worked on Pioneer 10, 11, and Voyager
  • 1980: Cosmos - A Personal Voyage aires on PBS
  • 1985: Writes "Contact", his best-selling novel
